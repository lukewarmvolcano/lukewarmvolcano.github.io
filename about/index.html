Fifteen Years Behind The Lens

Lukewarm Volcano is David Kelly's one-person studio, based in Kilrush, County Clare. Over fifteen years shooting and editing across Ireland, from brand films to music videos to community documentaries, he's built a style around natural light, honest performances, and giving every project the same close attention — whether you're launching a brand, sharing a story, or documenting a moment worth remembering.

Story-first direction, a cinematic look, fast turnaround, and Ireland-wide reach, all from one point of contact, start to finish.
