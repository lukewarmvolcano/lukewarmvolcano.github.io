Video Production Services

Lukewarm Volcano offers four core video services, each shot and edited personally by David Kelly from his studio in Kilrush, County Clare.

Business Videos (90 sec – 3 min): a professional business video for start-ups and long-standing companies alike, built around what makes your business worth talking about.

Product Videos (15–45 sec): eye-catching, memorable videos that build your brand and help sell your products.

Music Videos (3–5 min): for solo artists or bands, any genre, capturing the energy of your track.

Event Videos (full day coverage): from community launches to conferences, filmed as they happen and cut into a video that captures the day.
